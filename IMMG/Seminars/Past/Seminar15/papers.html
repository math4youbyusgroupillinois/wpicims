Submission of Full Papers


The authors whose papers were accepted on the basis of evaluation of the respective Abstracts are invited to submit their papers for publication in the Seminar Proceedings. Please note that in order to have your paper included in the Proceedings, it should be submitted no later than Friday, April 26, 2013.

The paper should be prepared and formatted in accordance with the instructions provided in the Seminar Paper's MS Word template - please be as precise as possible in following these instructions.
