Seminar Banquet

The Gala Dinner of the seminar participants (and their accompanying persons) will be held Wednesday evening, March 11, 2015 in Radovljica, the quiet and relaxing medieval town near Bled. The dinner will be preceded by a sightseeing tour including, in particular, the boat ride to the Lake Bled's island, visit to the island's church, a bus ride around the lake, visit of the Bled castle, and a city tour in Radovljica. The details of the tour and the dinner can be found on Seminar Banquet page (coming soon).

For the registered participants, as well as their accompanying persons, the Banquet ticket covering the entire sightseeing tour, all the related fees, an English-speaking local guide, and the cost of the dinner, will be 79 Euros.
