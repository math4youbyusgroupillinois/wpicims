The Toyota-RIT Applied Math Initiative
Connecting High School Mathematics to College and Careers





The Toyota-RIT Applied Math Initiative, (TAMI), is a program that runs workshops for high school mathematics teachers interested in learning more about how high school math is applied in professions and how high school math forms the basis for college curricula.

With funding from the Toyota Foundation, TAMI will run workshops at the Rochester Institute of Technology, University of Delaware, University of Minnesota, and Worcester Polytechnic Institute in 2014.

The Worcester Polytechnic Institute workshop will be Monday July 14th, 2014 - Thursday July 17th, 2014.
To register for the WPI program, fill out the online application form.
