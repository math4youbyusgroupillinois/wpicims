9th Seminar "Computer Modeling & Microwave Power Engineering"


March 5-6, 2007
Valencia, Spain

Endorsed by the International Microwave Power Institute (IMPI) and the Association for Microwave Power in Europe for Research and Education (AMPERE)

Sponsored by Escuela Técnica Superior de Ingenieros de Telecomunicación (the Telecommunication School) of Polytechnic University of Valencia

Seminar Chair: Prof. Vadim Yakovlev, WPI

Seminar Co-Chair: Prof. Jose M. Catala-Civera, Polytechnic University of Valencia, Valencia, Spain

Seminar Topics


  • microwave systems and components,
  • apparatuses for microwave thermal processes,
  • solution of EM-thermal coupled problems,
  • multiband/wideband antennas,
  • modeling of devices with ferrite materials,
  • computational reconstruction of material parameters,
  • numerical microwave optimization.

Accommodations


Special arrangement has been made for the seminar attendants in the hotel NH Ciudad de Valencia located in the seaport shopping/business district with easy access to the airport, the city railway station, and the harbor. The seminar site is within a 20 min. walk from the hotel; alternatively, it can be reached by a short city bus trip.

A major nearby attraction (a 15 min. walk from the hotel) is Ciudad de las Artes y las Ciencias (City of Arts and Sciences) - the famous ensemble designed by Valencian architect Santiago Calatrava and considered an impressive example of modern architecture. The "city" is made up of the opera house and performing arts centre, Imax cinema, planetarium, science museum, open-air oceanographic park, etc. The discounted rate for seminar participants is €60/night.
