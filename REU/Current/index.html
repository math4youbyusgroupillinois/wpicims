The 2013 REU in Industrial Mathematics and Statistics Program will be held Wednesday May 28, 2014 - Friday July 25, 2014.

The students are expected to arrive at WPI on Tuesday, May 27, 2014 and leave on Saturday, July 26, 2014.

The application deadline is Friday, February 28, 2014.

We will begin reviewing applications on Friday, February 14, 2014.

To apply to the program students need to:

  1. Fill out the on-line application form.
  2. E-mail to rpodell@wpi.edu a one-page essay describing your interest in participating in the WPI Program,
  3. Arrange to have two or three faculty complete the recommendation form, available in HTML or PDF versions.

Applicants must be undergraduates (usually students about to enter their senior or junior year) who will not graduate before December 2014. Applicants must be U.S. citizens or permanent residents. Exceptional applicants who do not satisfy these requirements will also be considered, provided the availability of additional funding from our industrial partners.



This program is funded the National Science Foundation REU Site program under Award DMS-1263127.
