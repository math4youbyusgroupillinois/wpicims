Facility Overview


ECKART GmbH is a leading international manufacturer of metallic pigments for the paints and coatings industry, lightweight concrete industries and the cosmetics industry. Drying and heat treatments are in many cases important steps within the processing chain. During the excursion the seminar participants will have the opportunity to view an 18 kW microwave dryer for glass pigments in operation. The dryer has been designed and manufactured by Püschner GmbH.
