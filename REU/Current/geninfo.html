General Information for 2014


Financial Support

We are able to provide a $4300 stipend and housing for the length of the program. We will also have some funds available to defray the cost of travel, and to support a limited number of students participate and present their REU research at conferences such as the annual Joint Mathematics Meeting.

Housing and Room Services

REU students will live in the East Hall apartments (marked "N" on the WPI campus map). These are apartment style residences, each with its own full service kitchen. Details can be found at http://www.wpi.edu/offices/rso/east-hall.html.

Campus Services

REU students will have access to all the campus facilities just like every other WPI student. They will be able to use all the athletic facilities, and will have full access to the Computing and Communications Center's resources.
