Accommodations


This year's Seminar is held as a Satellite Event to HES-13, the International Conference on Heating by Electromagnetic Sources.

For accommodation in Padua, Seminar participants are therefore advised to benefit from the related information provided by the organizers of HES-13.

Further Assistance


In case of any questions regarding accommodation in Padua, Seminar participants are encouraged to contact the Secretariat of HES-13, Iana Seleznova.
