Mathematics in Industry Institute for Teachers

2005 Summer Workshop

As part of the Mathematics in Industry Institute for Teachers, a summer Workshop will be organized at WPI on July 11-15, 2005.

During the workshop 50 teachers will work with faculty, industrial mathematicians and undergraduates on finding the best ways to use real-world problems to motivate students and in particular women and underrepresented minorities to pursue careers in mathematics, engineering, information technology or finance/economics.

Application

There is no fee for the workshop. A $500 stipend is provided to the workshop participants.

Housing

There are two options for housing. Please indicate your choice on the application form.

  • dormitory housing at WPI (double occupancy) at no charge to the participants.
    Rooms include towels, bedding (sheets, pillows, etc.), but these rooms are not air-conditioned.

  • the Courtyard Marriott which is about 10-15 minutes walk to the campus (air conditioning, cable TV, etc.)

Dorm rooms are reserved in Institute Hall (building G on the campus map). For more information you can call the Mathematical Sciences Department at (508)-831-5241 during between 7:30 am and 4:00 pm on weekdays, or the Campus Police at (508)-831-5433.
