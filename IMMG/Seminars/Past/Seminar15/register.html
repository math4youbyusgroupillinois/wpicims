Seminar Registration Fee

Please note that this year's Seminar will be held as a satellite event to HES-13, the International Conference on Heating by Electromagnetic Sources. As such, registrants for the Seminar who are not also registered for HES-13 will need to reimburse HES-13 for the use of the Seminar room and facilities, including lunch and coffee breaks. For this reason, the Seminar registration fee for these participants is higher.

For more information about HES-13, and to register for the HES-13 conference, please visit the conference home page.

The registration fee covering IMMG satellite Seminar technical sessions, Seminar materials, lunch, and coffee breaks is is:
