Accommodations

The Organizing Committee has reached an agreement with this hotel on special rates for the Seminar participants for the duration of the event and the adjacent dates. For sake of integrity, easiness of operations, and efficiency/productivity of networking, the Seminar organizers encourage all the attendees of the 17th Seminar to book their rooms in Hotel Lovec.

The hotel is located in the center of Bled, 100m from the lake and a 3 minute walk from the Union Bled bus station. Many details about Lovec's facilities, amenities, and services can be found on the Hotel's website and in its Factsheet.



A block of rooms is kept in Hotel Lovec for the seminar participants and their accompanying persons. To book a room, please fill in the reservation form and return it to Hotel Lovec (attn. to Mrs. Andreja Palhartinger) or by fax or e-mail (reservations@kompas-lovec.com) before February 11, 2015. (After that day, reservations can be made upon request if the rooms are still available.)
